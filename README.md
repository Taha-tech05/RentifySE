The vision of Rentify is to serve as a reliable, transparent, and user-friendly rental management platform that connects product owners and renters through a seamless, secure, and efficient digital experience. The platform simplifies the full lifecycle of rental activity, from listing and discovery all the way through to booking, return, and mutual review.
Product Owners use the system to manage their rental listings and track the full lifecycle of their products. They can add, update, and remove listings, monitor availability status, and receive notifications when renters return products. In Sprint 3, Owners also gained the ability to rate and review renters after a transaction is completed, contributing to a culture of accountability and trust on the platform.
Renters use the platform to browse available products, filter by category and price, view detailed listing information, check availability, make bookings, and now formally mark products as returned once a rental period has ended. The Return Product feature ensures that transaction records are updated accurately and that Owners are informed in real time. Renters can also leave ratings and reviews for Owners after completing a transaction, giving them an active voice in the platform's reputation system.
Admins use the system to oversee all platform activity with greater depth and detail in Sprint 3. Beyond moderating listings and monitoring transactions, Admins can now access a full tracking history that shows detailed records for every transaction, and can generate comprehensive reports covering rental activity, revenue summaries, and user behavior. These reports can be exported in PDF or Excel format, making it easy to analyze platform performance and share insights externally.
The Rentify Rental Management System has continued to evolve through a structured and iterative development process. Following the foundational work completed in Sprint 1 and the core feature development carried out in Sprint 2, Sprint 3 represents the final phase of the project where the system is brought to full completion as an integrated product.
Sprint 1 established the backbone of the platform through user authentication, registration, and role-based access control, allowing Owners, Renters, and Admins to securely interact with the system. Sprint 2 expanded the platform into a fully operational rental ecosystem by introducing product listing management, browsing and booking capabilities, admin moderation, and transaction monitoring. These two sprints collectively laid the groundwork for the advanced features introduced in Sprint 3.
Sprint 3 focused on completing the remaining use cases that close the rental lifecycle. This included the Rate and Review system allowing both Owners and Renters to evaluate each other after completed transactions, the Return Product flow enabling Renters to formally mark items as returned with owner notification and automatic record updates, and the Admin-side Tracking History and Report Generation features that provide comprehensive oversight and exportable insights into platform activity. With Sprint 3, the Rentify system reaches its intended scope as a complete, end-to-end rental management platform.
